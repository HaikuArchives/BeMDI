  CMDITitleView

CMDITitleView::HitTest
Returns the area the mouse is over.
virtual int32 HitTest(BPoint point);


Remarks
Returns one of the following values:
MDI_NC_CLOSE_BOX Mouse is over the close box.
MDI_NC_MAXIMIZE_BOX Mouse is over the maximize box.
MDI_NC_TITLE_TAB Mouse if over any other area of the title view.
