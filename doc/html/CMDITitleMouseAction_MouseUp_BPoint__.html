  CMDITitleMouseAction

CMDITitleMouseAction::MouseUp
Handles a mouse up event.
virtual void MouseUp(BPoint point);


Remarks
This method closes the target view and its attached MDI view, if the mouse is over the close box. If the mouse is over the maximize button, the attached MDI view is maximized.
